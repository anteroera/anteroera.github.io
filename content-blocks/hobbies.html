HOBBIES


HOBBIES|SNOWBOARDING

Actually, snowboarding has become more of a lifestyle than just a hobby for me during the past year and I've met many important friends while snowboarding. I started snowboarding shortly after I joined Skipoli, which is a skiing/snowboarding club at my home university, and immediately fell in love with it. Currently, I'm the vice president of Skipoli for season 16/17.



I love trips organized by other clubs as well as Skipolis trips, since I enjoy meeting new people who share my passion for snow. I have arranged several skiing trips myself, mostly within Finland but also a smaller trip to Austrian Alps as well. Some of these trips have had over 200 participants. In addition, I have been organizing various other events for Skipoli and events in cooperation with other academic skiing clubs.




HOBBIES|BOULDERING

Bouldering is the second of my two favorite sports. First I started with indoor top-rope, but I'm much more into bouldering nowadays. It is a very physical sport and also a very social one, despite of ultimately being an individual sport. You are never alone in a bouldering gym and the better will help you.



Bouldering challenges you to surpass yourself every time with new problems. The bouldering routes are called problems, because bouldering actually includes a very mental side for the sport and you need to plan ahead before wasting your power. There might be a very clever trick to solve some problems, and the feeling you get from finally figuring it out is the best there is.
