HOBBIES|SNOWBOARDING

Actually, snowboarding has become more of a lifestyle than just a hobby for me during the past year. I started snowboarding shortly after I joined Skipoli, which is a skiing/snowboarding club at my home university, and immediately fell in love with it. Currently, I'm in the board of Skipoli as the vice president for season 16/17.

I've made many new friends while snowboarding on various trips at different places. Very good friends from Skipoli and also a lot of friends from other universities skiing and snowboarding clubs. I love trips organized by other clubs as well as Skipolis trips, since I love meeting new people with the same passion in snow.

Snowboarding is a sport where you drive yourself to try out new things all the time! And as you go for new achievements, falling over and over again is inevitable. I have fallen countless of times and haven't remained without some injuries. But that's the nature of the sport and nothing can take away my joy for snowboarding!
